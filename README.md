## 🚀 Key Highlights & Core Thesis
* [cite_start]**Strategic AI Positioning**: Infosys has formalized a collaboration with Anthropic PBC to build enterprise AI solutions, differentiating itself in generative AI adoption[cite: 14].
* [cite_start]**Record Deal Pipeline**: The company secured its highest-ever Large Deal Total Contract Value (TCV) in FY25 at $11.6 billion (+56% net new), ensuring strong revenue visibility[cite: 13].
* [cite_start]**Exceptional Cash Generation**: Infosys reported a record Free Cash Flow (FCF) of $4.1 billion in FY25, representing a 41.8% year-over-year increase[cite: 15].
* [cite_start]**Margin Resilience**: Internal cost-optimization efforts under "Project Maximus" delivered a 50 basis point margin expansion in FY25[cite: 16].
* [cite_start]**Attractive Valuation Discount**: Trading at a 15.96x TTM P/E, Infosys offers a compelling valuation opportunity compared to Tier-1 peers like TCS (~24x) and HCL Tech (~22x)[cite: 17, 45].
* [cite_start]**Workforce Recovery**: The net addition of 15,246 employees across the first three quarters of FY26 signals growing demand confidence and operational recovery[cite: 84].

## 📊 Valuation Methodology
[cite_start]The 12-month target price of ₹1,520 is calculated using a blended valuation approach that equally weights three distinct methodologies[cite: 42]:

* [cite_start]**P/E Multiple (33% Weight)**: Yields an implied price of ₹1,545 based on an 18.5x multiple applied to FY27E EPS of ₹83.50[cite: 43].
* [cite_start]**Price-to-FCF (33% Weight)**: Yields an implied price of ₹1,560 based on a 20x multiple applied to FY27E FCF/share of ₹78[cite: 43].

## ⚠️ Key Risks Assessed
* [cite_start]**AI Disruption**: The potential for generative AI to automate software coding is mitigated by Infosys transitioning into an AI-augmented delivery model through its Anthropic partnership[cite: 49, 50].
* [cite_start]**Macroeconomic Headwinds**: A potential US economic slowdown could dampen IT deal ramps, as ~59% of revenues are generated in North America[cite: 52, 53].
* [cite_start]**Currency Fluctuations**: Margins remain vulnerable to INR appreciation against the USD and EUR, where a 1% INR appreciation erodes margins by ~30-40 bps[cite: 54, 55].
